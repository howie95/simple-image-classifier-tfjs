A Simple Image Classifier

What 🧐

This machine learning application is powered by Angular and TensorFlow.js, you can train a simple image classifier in your browser without backend support.

How 🤓

First of all, prepare your images, group them into classes.
I already preset some train data for you to use.
Then import and label them in the next step and press training button.
Well done, now you can playing with your trained classifier!

About 🤗

This project was made while learning TensorFlow.js, so the model is very simple and may not reliable🥺.
Also the project is inspired by the TeachableMachine and Lobe.ai
